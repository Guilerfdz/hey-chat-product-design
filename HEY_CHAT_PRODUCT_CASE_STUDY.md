# Hey! Chat — Product Case Study

Design of an alternative social architecture focused on meaningful interaction, emotional privacy and relational clarity.

---

## 1. Problem Context

Current social platforms share structural issues:

- Intimacy and exposure coexist in the same feed  
- Public metrics drive behavior  
- Systems optimize for attention, not relationships  
- Discovery is global, not contextual  

This leads to:

- Social fatigue  
- Anxiety and comparison  
- Shallow interactions  
- Loss of relational clarity  

---

## 2. Design Hypothesis

> Separating stable relationships from public exploration can reduce social friction and improve interaction quality.

---

## 3. Core Architecture

### Cercano (Close)

Space for stable relationships.

- Private chats  
- Small groups (Círculos)  
- Structured conversations (Threads)  
- Contextual updates  

Focus:
- Depth  
- Continuity  
- Trust  

---

### Lejano (Far)

Space for contextual discovery.

- Map-based exploration  
- Public local content  
- Events and live activity  
- Presence by location  

Focus:
- Exploration  
- Context  
- Low-pressure interaction  

No global infinite feed.

---

## 4. Public Visibility Model

- No automatic global exposure  
- Visibility depends on context (location)  
- Content has limited reach  
- Discovery does not override relationships  

---

## 5. Bidirectional Interaction System

Problem:  
Most social systems allow unilateral actions.

Solution:  
Consent-based interaction model.

- Requests require acceptance  
- Features unlock progressively  
- Sensitive actions require confirmation  
- Relationships can pause without penalty  

---

## 6. Social Structure (MA / FAM)

### MA (Individual Bond)

- Mutual connection  
- Private shared space  
- Symbolic interaction  

### FAM (Small Group)

- 3–6 people  
- Invitation-based  
- Shared identity  
- Protected environment  

---

## 7. Expression & Economy

- Visual identity over status  
- No public metrics  
- Symbolic progression  
- Personal expression tools  

---

## 8. Reputation Path

- Based on behavior, not popularity  
- Measures consistency and interaction quality  
- Unlocks features progressively  

---

## 9. Experimental Systems

### Ghost Eye

Preview messages before sending.

Goal:  
Reduce impulsive communication and anxiety.

---

### Music Rooms / JAM

Ephemeral shared spaces for real-time presence and creativity.

---

## 10. Metrics Approach

Instead of:

- Time spent  
- Reach  
- Virality  

Focus on:

- Conversation depth  
- Relationship duration  
- Bidirectional interaction  
- Retention within small groups  

---

## 11. Risks

- Slower growth  
- Less viral potential  
- Requires critical mass locally  
- More complex concept  

---

## 12. Status

- Concept defined  
- UX exploration in progress  
- Prototype phase starting
