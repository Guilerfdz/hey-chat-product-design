# Hey! Chat

A social platform concept focused on how we communicate, not just what we share.

---

## Overview

Hey! Chat explores alternative social architectures designed to reduce digital fatigue and improve relational quality.

Instead of maximizing attention, the system prioritizes:

- Meaningful interactions  
- Emotional privacy  
- Contextual social discovery  
- Reduced cognitive overload  

---

## Core Idea

> What if social platforms were designed to strengthen relationships instead of capturing attention?

Hey! Chat proposes a dual-layer architecture:

- **Cercano (Close)** → stable relationships and communication  
- **Lejano (Far)** → contextual discovery based on location and activity  

This separation reduces ambiguity between intimacy and exposure.

---

## Key Concepts

- Bidirectional social interactions (consent-based)  
- No infinite global feed  
- Contextual visibility instead of mass exposure  
- Social structures based on trust, not popularity
